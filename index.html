☕ Adrak Chai ▼
"Each breath clears space for your best thinking."
🌿 adrak chai recipe ↓

Ingredients (1 cup)

  • 1 cup water
  • ½ cup whole milk
  • 1 tsp CTC tea / 2 tsp loose leaf
  • 1-inch fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp jaggery or sugar
  • Pinch of black pepper

Method

  • Boil water with ginger
  • Add tea, simmer 2 min
  • Pour in milk, bring to boil
  • Sweeten, strain & sip slowly ☕
